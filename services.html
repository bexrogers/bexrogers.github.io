Descriptions of Services

LinkedIn Revamp

I will conduct a full LinkedIn audit from top to bottom and help you find endorsements, connections, and recs.

Resume Writing and Editing

The final resume will be one page, grammatically-sound, include a brand statement, be ATS-friendly, have well-organized headings, make use of outcomes-oriented achievements rather than tasks, have a polished skills and education section, and will be oriented to your industries of interest.

Cover Letter Writing and Editing

I use a cover letter writing structure and strategy that is aimed at addressing everything a recruiter needs to know. You'll have an intro, a paragraph addressing your past applicable experiences, in an achievement-oriented tone, a third paragraph focused on why you're a great fit for the company, and a closing paragraph where you ask for an interview.

Mock Interview Session

We'll have a 30 minute mock interview session, going over behavioral questions, as well as technical questions for certain fields. With your permission, the interview will be recorded, and I will analyze it for improvements.

Portfolio Consultation

Some fields, including software engineering, data science, and design require portfolios. There are wrong and right ways to build an effective portfolio; whether you have a completed portfolio or haven't started, we'll get it to a point where it's ready for recruiters to see. I work with HTML and CSS if your portfolio requires front-end changes.

Networking Prep Session

Networking is a major piece of the job hunt. LinkedIn is a great tool, but you'll have even better luck if you attend events (likely virtual for now). We'll discuss how best to network and address any insecurities or imposter syndrome.

Customized Networking Plan

If you're looking for more support with networking, I'll build out a catered weekly schedule of events for you to attend and help you write follow-ups, thank you notes, and plan future interactions with connections.

Time Management Planning

Everything I've said thus far means nothing without a time management plan. If you're unemployed or just out of school, it's especially important that you have a great time management system. If you're working full time, you'll need to find time to focus on the job search. We'll use tools such as ToDoist, Trello, and the Pomodoro method to get you on track and help you stay disiplined.

Start-To-Finish Full Package

If you want someone with you for every step of this process, who is always a text, email, or phone call away, we can make that happen.

Custom Plan

If you're looking for a combination of services, let's set up a phone call.
